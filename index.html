Spring 2026 · Electromagnetics / EE3605

Quarter-Wave Transformer Micro-strip — CST Studio Suite

Designed and simulated a quarter-wave micro-strip impedance matching network on a flexible polyimide substrate (εr=3.2) at 0.966 GHz. Calculated effective dielectric constant, guided wavelength, transformer impedance (63.96 Ω), and trace widths from transmission-line equations. Evaluated impedance matching via S11 S-parameter analysis under both flat and bent substrate conditions to study how mechanical deformation affects RF performance.

Spring 2026 · Electromagnetics / EE3605

Near-Field Wireless Power Transfer — CST Studio Suite

Modeled a two-coil inductive wireless power transfer system in CST Studio Suite using the Low Frequency (LF) solver at 114 kHz (λ ≈ 2,632 m — near-field magnetoquasistatic regime). Designed Tx coil (31 mm, 10 turns) and Rx coil (15 mm, 5 turns) from student-ID-derived parameters. Configured gap sweeps (4–10 mm) and frequency sweeps (94–134 kHz) to analyze magnetic flux density |B| and field intensity |H| at the receiver center.
